# The Stained Guard's Shadow

The Kingdom of Krusen is cursed. A defunct order of knights lies in forgotten tombs. And a new group of adventurers seeks fortune, truth, and perhaps a new beginning.

<details>
<summary>The Accidental Heroes</summary>

* **Skutch Henks:** A rogue living on borrowed time, bound to an infernal mirror that demands gold to turn back the clock.
* **Errhesh Domash:** A towering Dragonborn barbarian who would rather be a scribe, currently building his resume.
* **D'Blorbus Or'ghouls:** An Aasimar Sorcerer marked by a corrupted celestial blessing.
* **Ash:** A wandering human monk and woodcarver who simply went with the flow.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Current Objective: Low Mountain</summary>

* **The Mission:** Eliminate the goblin threat plaguing Wagonwood and investigate the mysteries surrounding the Stained Guard.
* **Recent Events:** Ambushed a goblin raiding party, slaughtered a rival adventuring group, and claimed the Helm of the Blood Horn. Currently navigating the dense forest toward the crater of Low Mountain.

</details>
