# House Vinchel

**Roles:** Local Nobility  
**Location:** Wagonwood

## Baron Benjamin Vinchel

* The ruling authority in the Wagonwood region of the Kingdom of Cruces. 
* Tasked with maintaining order and handling the growing goblin threat, though his resources are stretched thin.

## Stuart Vinchel

* **The Rescued Son:** Recently kidnapped by goblins and taken to the Tomb of the Stained Guard. 
* **Current Status:** Rescued by the party. Thanks to Skutch's intimidation, Stuart is publicly telling a highly exaggerated tale of his own heroism during the rescue.
