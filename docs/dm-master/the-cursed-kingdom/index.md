# The Stained Guard's Shadow

The Kingdom of Crusen is cursed. A defunct order of knights lies in forgotten tombs. And a new group of adventurers seeks fortune, truth, and perhaps a new beginning.

<details>
<summary>The Accidental Heroes</summary>

* **Skutch Henks:** A rogue living on borrowed time, bound to an infernal mirror that demands servitude to turn back the clock.
* **Errhesh Domash:** A towering Dragonborn barbarian who has become an adventurer for the money.
* **D'Blorbus Or'ghouls:** An Aasimar Sorcerer marked by a corrupted celestial blessing.
* **Ash:** A wandering human monk who simply went with the flow.

</details>
