# Wagonwood

**Region:** Kingdom of Cruces, Continent of Aethelgard  
**Current Authority:** Baron Benjamin Vinchel

## Overview

A rugged, woodland settlement currently plagued by an escalating threat from local goblin tribes. The dense forests hide old ruins and forgotten history from before the Wild Magic Storms.

## Key Locations

* **The Vinchel Estate:** The seat of local power.
* **The Magic Shop:** The only place to find magic items.
