# The Shattered Balance

A historical record of the heroes who stood against the failing of the cosmic lock and the return of the dark.

<details>
<summary>The Heroes of the Age</summary>

* **Alrick:** Avatar of Noctus, channeling the survival domain of a dead god.
* **Balnan:** The Time-Locked Fail-safe.
* **13:** Master of the arcane arts and pure Old Magic.
* **Aukan:** The Avenger, striking against the extremists of the Order.
* **Darkscale:** The Infiltrator and consumer of essence.

</details>

<details>
<summary>The Legacy Left Behind</summary>

* **The Storms Calmed:** The Wild Magic Storms tearing the fabric of reality were neutralized, leading to a more stable, albeit magic-scarce, world.
* **The Lorkhaj Restored:** The destruction of the Lich's Phylactery released the trapped souls, allowing the Lorkhaj to return to the natural cycle.
* **The Order Reborn:** From the ashes of extremism, a new Order of All was forged to protect the realm.

</details>
